Privacy Policy - RUSTSKINDEX

Last Updated: October 18, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how RUSTSKINDEX uses and protects the information you provide when using our application.

1. What information do we collect?

When you use our application, we collect the following information:

  • Your SteamID, if you choose to enter it. This information is kept locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

2. How do we use the information we collect?

We collect your SteamID in order to:

  • Display your Steam inventory, if it is public.
  • Provide you with information about Rust game skins and their value on the Steam market.

3. Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect and secure the information we collect online.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

  • Advertising: Our application may contain advertisements. Third-party advertisers may collect information when you interact with their ads. We have no control over these third parties.
  • Third-Party Links: Our application may contain links to third-party services, such as the Steam Market. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Control of your personal information

Since our application only collects your SteamID, which remains local on your phone, you always have control. You can choose not to enter your SteamID, which will simply prevent access to your Steam inventory through our app.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at:
mycodeapps@gmail.com
