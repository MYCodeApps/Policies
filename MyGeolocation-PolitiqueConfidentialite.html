Privacy Policy - My Geolocation

Last Updated: February 15, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how My Geolocation uses and protects the information you provide when using our application.

1. What information do we collect?

When you use our application, we may collect the following information:

  • Your geographical position
  • Your device's location data

2. How do we use the information we collect?

We collect this information to understand your needs and provide a better service, specifically for the following reasons:

  • To share your location with your contacts
  • To personalize your experience based on your preferences

3. Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect and secure the information we collect online.

4. Advertising

Please note that our application may contain advertising. Third-party advertisers may collect information when you interact with their ads. These third parties may include advertising agencies and networks. We have no control over these third parties and their use of your information.

5. Links to Google Maps

Our application may contain links to Google Maps. Please note that Google Maps is governed by Google's privacy policies. By using these features, you are subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Control of your personal information

Since our application does not collect identifiable personal information (such as name or address), you do not need to worry about restricting its collection. However, you maintain control over your device's location function through your phone settings or by uninstalling the app.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us at:
mycodeapps@gmail.com
